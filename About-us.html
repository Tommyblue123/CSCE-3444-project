About Us

Our Mission

At Share the Spare, we're driven by a single goal: to make a meaningful impact in the fight against food waste and hunger. In a world where millions go hungry every day, yet vast amounts of food are wasted, we saw an opportunity to bridge the gap. Our mission is to create a more sustainable future by connecting food establishments that have surplus food with local charities and individuals facing food scarcity.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Tommy, Share the Spare started as a small community effort in different communities. Recognizing the critical issue of food wastage in restaurants, cafes, supermarkets, and other food-related businesses, we embarked on a journey to change the narrative. What began as a local initiative has grown into a widespread movement, expanding our reach and impact thanks to the dedication of our team, volunteers, and the generous support of our community.

What We Do

Our platform offers an easy-to-use solution for food establishments to Donated their surplus food at the end of each day. We partner with a network of local charities, shelters, and individuals in need, ensuring that the food serves its purpose - to nourish and sustain. Through our efforts, we aim to:

  • Reduce the environmental impact of food waste
  • Alleviate hunger in our communities
  • Promote a culture of giving and shared responsibility

Our Impact

Since our inception, Share the Spare has successfully redirected 1,000,000 pounds of food from landfills to dinner tables. We've partnered with over 10,000 food establishments and supported 10,000 local charities and thousands of individuals in need. But our work is far from over. With every meal shared, we're one step closer to a hunger-free, sustainable world.
