How It Works

At Share the Spare, we're committed to making the donation and acquisition of surplus food as straightforward and efficient as possible. Our platform bridges the gap between food establishments with excess food and local charities or individuals facing food scarcity. Here's how our process works:

For Food Establishments

  1. Sign Up and List Your Surplus
    • Register your food establishment on our platform.
    • Easily list surplus food items at the end of each day through our user-friendly dashboard or app
  2. Connect with Local Charities
    • Once your surplus is listed, local charities and individuals in need can view your donation.
    • Receive notifications when your donation is claimed.
  3. Coordinate Pickup or Delivery
    • Coordinate directly with the recipients for pickup or opt for our delivery assistance, where available.
    • Feel proud, knowing your surplus food is directly helping those in need rather than going to waste.

For Charities and Individuals

  1. Register and Browse Listings
    • Sign up as a charity or individual recipient.
    • Browse available surplus food listings from local establishments in real-time.
  2. Claim Your Donation
    • Claim surplus food directly through the platform.
    • Communicate with donors to ask any questions about the food items.
  3. Arrange for Pickup or Delivery
    • Organize a time to pick up the food from the donor.
    • Optionally, request delivery assistance if offered by the donor or our platform.

Making an Impact Together

With just a few simple steps, Share the Spare facilitates a meaningful connection between those with too much and those with too little. Our platform not only combats food waste but also plays a critical role in alleviating hunger within our communities.
Join us today and be part of the solution. Whether you're a food establishment with surplus to share or someone in need of food assistance, Share the Spare is here to connect and support our community every step of the way.
